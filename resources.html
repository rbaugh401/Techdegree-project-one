Useful tools and sites

Programs

In my work as a graphic designer, I've learned about a program similar to the Adobe Suite that does not require a subscription. Affinity has their Photo, Designer, and Publisher programs which took little adaptation to adjust to from the Adobe suites. Plus, they are only $50 each, one-time.

Vectors and Images

Here are some of the sites I use regularly to find high-quality graphics for free or a reasonable subscription:

  • Freepik.com
  • Pixabay.com
  • Unsplash.com
  • Pexels.com

These are great for when I need a good image but don't want to take one myself.
